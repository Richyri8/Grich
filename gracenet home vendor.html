How to List Your Products on Gracenet Marketplace as a Vendor

Listing your product on Gracenet is simple, fast, and completely results-based. We connect you directly with motivated affiliates who are ready to promote your offers — and you only pay when you make a sale.

🪜 Step-by-Step Guide

1. Sign Up Free & List Your Product

Create a free vendor account on the Gracenet app and list your digital product with all required information.

2. Pay Only When You Get Results

No upfront listing fees. You only pay commissions to affiliates when they successfully sell your product.

3. Watch the Sales Roll In

Once approved, your product goes live in our marketplace. Affiliates start promoting immediately, and you start earning.

4. Connect With Affiliates Eager to Promote

Gracenet connects you with a growing network of affiliates who are actively seeking high-converting products to promote.

📋 What Are the Details Needed to List Your Product?

  • Name of Vendor
  • Name of Product
  • Sales Page Link
    This is compulsory. If you don’t have a sales page, please hire a copywriter or email hire@gracenet.com to work with one of our in-house writers.
  • Product Delivery Process (Access Link)
  • Price of the Product
  • Commission Offers
    State the percentage affiliates will earn per sale. Higher commissions attract more affiliates.
  • Customer Support Information
  • Promotional Materials
  • Link to Sales Challenge Page
  • Vendor’s Personal Email Address

⏳ Approval Timeline

After submission, our team will review your product and perform quality checks. Approval typically takes 2–5 working days.
